About UNPLUGGED 2.0

UNPLUGGED 2.0 is a hardware hackathon focusing on creating innovative tech solutions with sustainability at the forefront. Hosted by IETE-SF, a dynamic committee of Dwarkadas J Sanghvi College of Engineering's EXTC department, this event is the most awaited weekend of the year.

Innovative Challenges

Tackle real-world problems with innovative hardware solutions. Beat the clock, and there's brownie points for being eco-friendly.

Networking Opportunities

Connect with industry experts and like-minded innovators, including some of our own alumini!

Exciting Prizes

A whooping Rs 50,000 is awarded to the winners, Rs 25,000 to the first runner ups, and Rs 7000 to the second runner ups.
